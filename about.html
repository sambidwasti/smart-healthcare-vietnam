About the Smart ICT Healthcare Center

The Smart ICT Healthcare Center is a transformative initiative dedicated to enhancing healthcare access in rural and underserved regions of Vietnam. It integrates digital technologies—including telemedicine, electronic health records (EHR), and maternal health alert systems—to support local clinics and improve care delivery.

By enabling real-time consultations, digitizing patient data, and sending timely health alerts, the center empowers healthcare workers with modern tools and ensures better health outcomes for the community. This project represents a sustainable step toward bridging healthcare disparities using smart, scalable ICT solutions.
