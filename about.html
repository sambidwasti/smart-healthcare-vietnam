About the Smart ICT Healthcare Center

The Smart ICT Healthcare Center is a digital initiative aimed at improving access to medical services in underserved and rural areas of Vietnam. By integrating technologies such as telemedicine, electronic health records (EHR), and automated maternal alerts, the project empowers local clinics and health workers to deliver faster, smarter, and more reliable healthcare solutions.

Through real-time remote consultations, secure digital records, and timely notifications for expectant mothers, this platform addresses key challenges in modern rural healthcare and enhances community well-being using sustainable digital innovation.
